Content Policy

Last Updated: November 30, 2025

MyToob is committed to providing a safe and respectful experience for all users. This Content Policy outlines our guidelines for content displayed within the app and the tools available to you for managing your viewing experience.

YouTube Content

MyToob displays videos from YouTube using the official YouTube IFrame Player API. All YouTube content is subject to YouTube's own Community Guidelines and Terms of Service.

Reporting Inappropriate Content

If you encounter content on YouTube that violates YouTube's Community Guidelines, you can report it directly to YouTube:

  1. Right-click (or Control-click) on any video in MyToob
  2. Select "Report Content" from the context menu
  3. You'll be taken to YouTube's reporting page where you can submit your report

YouTube reviews all reports and takes appropriate action according to their policies.

Hiding Channels

You have control over which channels appear in your MyToob library:

  1. Right-click (or Control-click) on any video from a channel you wish to hide
  2. Select "Hide Channel" from the context menu
  3. Optionally provide a reason (for your own records)
  4. Confirm the action

Hidden channels can be managed from the sidebar under "YouTube" â†’ "Manage Hidden Channels". You can unhide channels at any time.

Local Video Files

MyToob allows you to import and organize local video files stored on your Mac. You are solely responsible for ensuring that any local files you import comply with applicable laws and do not infringe on others' rights.

Important: MyToob does not download, cache, or store any YouTube video or audio content. YouTube videos are streamed directly through the official YouTube player. Only video metadata (titles, descriptions, thumbnails) is cached locally to improve your experience.

AI-Powered Features

MyToob uses on-device AI to help organize and discover videos:

  • Video Recommendations: Based on your viewing history and preferences
  • Topic Clustering: Automatically groups similar videos together
  • Semantic Search: Find videos by describing what you're looking for

All AI processing happens entirely on your device. No video content, viewing history, or personal data is sent to external servers for AI analysis.

Prohibited Uses

You agree not to use MyToob to:

  • Circumvent YouTube's content restrictions or access controls
  • Download, rip, or extract YouTube video or audio streams
  • Remove, disable, or bypass advertisements shown in YouTube videos
  • Scrape, harvest, or collect data from YouTube beyond normal viewing
  • Violate any applicable laws or third-party rights
