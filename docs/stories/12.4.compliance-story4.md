# Story 12.4: Support & Contact Information

## Status
Done ✅

## Story
**As a** user,
**I want** easily accessible support contact information,
**so that** I can report issues or ask questions.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. ✅ "Support" or "Contact" link in Settings > About section
2. ✅ Contact options provided: email, support page URL
3. ✅ "Send Diagnostics" button creates sanitized log archive and opens email client
4. ✅ Support email response time commitment stated
5. ✅ FAQ or Help Center link provided
6. ✅ Support information shown in App Store listing (consistent with in-app)
7. ✅ UI test verifies support links are accessible from Settings
